The Best Movies According to Chainalyn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inside Out

Explores the emotions inside an girl's mind as she navigates a major life change. With its imaginative storytelling, emotional depth, and stunning visuals, the movie offers a unique perspective on how feelings like Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, Anxiety, embarrassment, and Disgust shape our experiences. It's a touching and insightful journey for viewers of all ages.

Forgotten

Is a South Korean psychological thriller packed with twists and suspense. The story follows Jin-seok as he unravels the mystery behind his brother's strange disappearance and sudden return with a completely different personality. The movie is highly praised for its unexpected revelations, emotional depth, and masterful storytelling.

Unlocked

Is a South Korean thriller that delves into the dangers of modern technology. The story follows a young woman whose life spirals out of control after losing her smartphone, which ends up in the hands of a manipulative hacker. With its gripping narrative, sharp commentary on privacy, and intense suspense, the movie keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
